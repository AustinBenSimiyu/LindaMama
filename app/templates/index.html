About Zawadi Care

Zawadi Care is dedicated to supporting expectant mothers in Kenya and Sub-Saharan Africa. Our mission is to provide a safe, educational, and empowering space for every pregnancy journey. With trusted information, tools, and access to healthcare professionals, we help you make informed decisions for you and your baby.

Safety. Education. Empowerment.
